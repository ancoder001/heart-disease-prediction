About Heart Disease

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide. Early prediction using Machine Learning can help improve diagnosis and treatment.

Symptoms

  • Chest Pain
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Fatigue
  • Dizziness
  • Irregular Heartbeat

Risk Factors

  • High Blood Pressure
  • High Cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Diabetes
  • Obesity

Prevention

  • Healthy Diet
  • Exercise
  • No Smoking
  • Regular Checkups
  • Stress Management
